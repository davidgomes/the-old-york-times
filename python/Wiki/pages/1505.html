Year 1505 (MDV) was a common year starting on Wednesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • June 19 Zhengde Emperor ascends the throne of the Ming Dynasty.
    • \n
  • June 27 \xe2\x80\x93 The future King Henry VIII of England repudiates his engagement to Catherine of Aragon, at his father\'s command.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 2 \xe2\x80\x93 Martin Luther, then 22, vows to become a monk in a moment of terror, due to a near lightning strike during a thunderstorm, near the village of Stotternheim.
    • \n
  • July 17 \xe2\x80\x93 Luther enters the monastic life at an Augustinian cloister in Erfurt.
    • \n
  • July 24 \xe2\x80\x93 Travelling to India, a group of Portuguese explorers sack the city-state of Kilwa in East Africa, killing the king for failing to pay tribute.
    • \n
  • November 6 \xe2\x80\x93 Vasili III succeeds Ivan III as Grand Prince of Muscovy.
    • \n
  • December 18 \xe2\x80\x93 John IX van Horne, prince-bishop of Li\xc3\xa8ges, Belgium, is executed.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • A Portuguese fleet attacks Kilwa and then Mombasa. The Portuguese then attempt to monopolize the trade in the east African ports, but are unable to maintain control (by the late 16th century, Swahili groups regain control of several ports from the Portuguese).
    • \n
  • Portuguese merchants establish factories on the east coast of Africa.
    • \n
  • Portuguese under Dom Louren\xc3\xa7o d\'Almeida reach Colombo, Sri Lanka, and send envoys to King of Kotte; they also reach the Comoros.
    • \n
  • Bermuda is discovered by Spanish explorer Juan de Berm\xc3\xbadez.
    • \n
  • King Alexander of Poland signs the Nihil novi Act, making Poland a Nobles\' Democracy.
    • \n
  • Poland prohibits peasants from leaving their lands, establishing serfdom.
    • \n
  • Christ\'s College, Cambridge, is re-founded, receiving its charter from Lady Margaret Beaufort.
    • \n
  • Judah Abravanel becomes personal physician to the viceroy of Naples.
    • \n
  • Battle of Achnashellach said to have taken place in Scotland.
    • \n
  • A Massive 8.8 Magnitude earthquake struck Nepal causing severe damage in Kathmandu, Western Nepal and some parts of Indo-Gangetic plain.
    • \n
  • 1505 or 1506 - Portuguese explorer Gon\xc3\xa7alo \xc3\x81lvares is the first to sight what will later be known as Gough Island in the South Atlantic.
    • \n
\n
