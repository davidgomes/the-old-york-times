\n1691 (MDCXCI) was a common year starting on Monday (dominical letter G) of the Gregorian calendar and a common year starting on Thursday (dominical letter D) of the Julian calendar, the 1691st year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 691st year of the 2nd millennium, the 91st year of the 17th century, and the 2nd year of the 1690s decade. Note that the Julian day for 1691 is 10 calendar days difference, which continued to be used from 1582 until the complete conversion of the Gregorian calendar was entirely done in 1929.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • March 5 \xe2\x80\x93 Nine Years\' War: French troops under Marshal Louis-Francois de Boufflers besiege the Spanish-held town of Mons.
    • \n
  • March 20 \xe2\x80\x93 Leisler\'s Rebellion: A new governor arrives in New York \xe2\x80\x93 Jacob Leisler surrenders after a standoff of several hours.
    • \n
  • March 29 \xe2\x80\x93 The Siege of Mons ends in the city\xe2\x80\x99s surrender.
    • \n
  • April 9 \xe2\x80\x93 A fire at the Palace of Whitehall in London destroys its Stone Gallery.
    • \n
  • May 6 \xe2\x80\x93 The Spanish inquisition condemns and forcibly baptizes 219 xuetas in Palma, Majorca. When 37 try to escape the island, they are burned alive at the stake.
    • \n
  • May 16 \xe2\x80\x93 Jacob Leisler is hanged for treason.
    • \n
  • June 23 \xe2\x80\x93 Ahmed II (1691\xe2\x80\x931695) succeeds Suleiman II (1687\xe2\x80\x931691) as emperor of the Ottoman Empire.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 12\n
      \n
    • Pope Innocent XII becomes the 242nd pope, succeeding Pope Alexander VIII.
      • \n
    • Williamite War in Ireland: Battle of Aughrim: Protestant Williamite forces led by Godert de Ginkell decisively defeat Jacobites under the Marquis de St Ruth (who is killed).
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • September 18 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Leuze: English and Dutch forces defeated by the French in the War of the Grand Alliance.
    • \n
  • October 3 \xe2\x80\x93 The Treaty of Limerick, ending the Williamite War in Ireland and guaranteeing civil rights to Roman Catholics, is signed. (It was broken "before the ink was dry") The Flight of the Wild Geese \xe2\x80\x94 the departure of the Jacobite army \xe2\x80\x94 follows.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • Michel Rolle invents Rolle\'s theorem, an essential theorem of mathematics.
    • \n
  • In New England the two separate colonies of Massachusetts Bay Colony and Plymouth Colony are united into a single entity by an act of the King and Queen of England.
    • \n
  • The Khalkha submit to the Manchu invaders, bringing most of modern-day Mongolia under the rule of the Qing dynasty.
    • \n
