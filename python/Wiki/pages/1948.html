1948 (MCMXLVIII) was a leap year starting on Thursday (dominical letter DC) of the Gregorian calendar, the 1948th year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 948th year of the 2nd millennium, the 48th year of the 20th century, and the 9th year of the 1940s decade.

Events[edit]

January[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 1\n
      \n
    • General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT) inaugurated.
      • \n
    • Railways of Britain are nationalized to form British Railways.
      • \n
    • The Constitution of the Italian Republic goes into effect.
      • \n
    • The latest Constitution of New Jersey goes into effect.
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • January 4 \xe2\x80\x93 Burma gains its independence from the United Kingdom, becoming an independent republic, named the Union of Burma, with Sao Shwe Thaik as its first President and U Nu its first Prime Minister.
    • \n
  • January 5 \xe2\x80\x93 Warner Brothers shows the first color newsreel (Tournament of Roses Parade and the Rose Bowl).
    • \n
  • January 7 \xe2\x80\x93 Kentucky Air National Guard pilot Thomas Mantell crashes while in pursuit of a supposed UFO.
    • \n
  • January 12 \xe2\x80\x93 Mahatma Gandhi begins his fast-unto-death in Delhi to stop communal violence during the Partition of India.
    • \n
  • January 15 \xe2\x80\x93 Ronnie Van Zant was born.
    • \n
  • January 17 \xe2\x80\x93 A truce is declared between nationalist Indonesian and Dutch troops in Java.
    • \n
  • January 22 \xe2\x80\x93 British foreign secretary Ernest Bevin proposes the formation of a Western Union between Britain, France and the Benelux countries to stand up against the Soviet Union. The Treaty of Brussels is signed March 17 as a consequence, a predecessor to NATO.
    • \n
  • January 26 \xe2\x80\x93 Teigin poison case: a man masquerading as a doctor poisons 12 of 16 bank employees of the Tokyo branch of Imperial Bank and takes the money; artist Sadamichi Hirasawa is later sentenced to death for the crime, but is never executed.
    • \n
  • January 29\n
      \n
    • The Pakistan Socialist Party is founded in Karachi.
      • \n
    • DC-3 aircraft crash at Los Gatos Creek, near Coalinga, California, kills four US citizens and 28 deportees, commemorated in a protest song ("Deportee (Plane Wreck at Los Gatos)") by Woody Guthrie.
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • January 30\n
      \n
    • Assassination of Mahatma Gandhi: Indian pacifist and leader Mahatma Gandhi is shot by Nathuram Godse in New Delhi. Also on this day Orville Wright of the pioneering aviators the Wright Brothers dies in Dayton, Ohio.
      • \n
    • 1948 Winter Olympics open in St. Moritz, Switzerland.
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • January 31 \xe2\x80\x93 The British crown colony of the Malayan Union, Penang and Malacca form the Federation of Malaya.[1]
    • \n

Aruba presents the mandate of the Aruban People for Aruba to become an Independent Country, under the souvereignty of the House of Orange, based on Aruba\'s first state constitution presented officially since August 1947, and a (4th) Member State of the future Dutch Commonwealth.

      \n
