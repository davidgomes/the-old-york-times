Year 1550 (MDL) was a common year starting on Wednesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 6 \xe2\x80\x93 Spanish Captain Hernando de Santana founds the city of Valledupar in what is now Colombian territory.
    • \n
  • February 8 \xe2\x80\x93 Pope Julius III succeeds Pope Paul III as the 221st pope.
    • \n
  • March 12 \xe2\x80\x93 Several hundred Spanish and indigenous troops under the command of Pedro de Valdivia defeat an army of 60,000 Mapuche at the Battle of Penco during the Arauco War in present-day Chile.
    • \n
  • June 12 \xe2\x80\x93 The city of Helsinki, Finland (belonging to Sweden at the time) is founded by King Gustav I of Sweden.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 21 \xe2\x80\x93 The Society of Jesus (Jesuits) is approved by Pope Julius III.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • Altan Khan besieges Peking.
    • \n
  • Iceland becomes fully Protestant.
    • \n
  • The first grammatical description of the French language is published by Louis Maigret.
    • \n
  • The first book in Slovene, Catechismus, written by Protestant reformer Primo\xc5\xbe Trubar, is printed in Schw\xc3\xa4bisch Hall, Holy Roman Empire.
    • \n
  • Nostradamus\' first almanac is written.
    • \n
  • John Dee finishes his studies at Katholieke Universiteit Leuven.
    • \n
  • Sherborne School is established.
    • \n
  • Navigable summit level canal completed between Alster and the Trave.
    • \n
  • Approximate date \xe2\x80\x93 The discovery of silver at Zacatecas and Guanajuato in Mexico, and Potos\xc3\xad in Bolivia, stimulates silver rushes.
    • \n
