1934 (MCMXXXIV) was a common year starting on Monday (dominical letter G) of the Gregorian calendar, the 1934th year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 934th year of the 2nd millennium, the 34th year of the 20th century, and the 5th year of the 1930s decade.

January[edit]

\n
Main article: January 1934
\n
    \n
  • January 1 \xe2\x80\x93 International Telecommunication Union established.
    • \n
  • January 7 \xe2\x80\x93 The Flash Gordon comic strip is first published, in the United States.
    • \n
  • January 15 \xe2\x80\x93 The 8.0 Mw Nepal\xe2\x80\x93Bihar earthquake strikes Nepal and Bihar with a maximum Mercalli intensity of XI (Extreme), killing an estimated 6,000\xe2\x80\x9310,700 people.
    • \n
  • January 20 \xe2\x80\x93 The Japanese company Fuji Photo Film is established.
    • \n
  • January 26 \xe2\x80\x93 The 10 year German\xe2\x80\x93Polish Non-Aggression Pact is signed by Nazi Germany and the Second Polish Republic.
    • \n
  • January 30 \xe2\x80\x93 In Nazi Germany the political power of the federal states such as Prussia is substantially abolished by the "Law on the Reconstruction of the Reich" (Gesetz \xc3\xbcber den Neuaufbau des Reiches).
    • \n
