1931 (MCMXXXI) was a common year starting on Thursday (dominical letter D) of the Gregorian calendar, the 1931st year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 931st year of the 2nd millennium, the 31st year of the 20th century, and the 2nd year of the 1930s decade.

April[edit]

\n
Main article: April 1931
\n
    \n
  • April 1 \xe2\x80\x93 The Second Encirclement Campaign against Jiangxi Soviet in China is launched by the Kuomintang government to destroy the Communist forces in Jiangxi province.
    • \n
  • April 6 \xe2\x80\x93 The Portuguese government declares martial law in Madeira and in the Azores because of an attempted military takeover in Funchal.
    • \n
  • April 9 \xe2\x80\x93 Argentinian anarchist Severino Digiovanni is executed.
    • \n
  • April 12 \xe2\x80\x93 Municipal elections in Spain, which are treated as a virtual referendum on the monarchy, result in the triumph for the republican parties.
    • \n
  • April 14 \xe2\x80\x93 The Second Spanish Republic is proclaimed in Madrid. Meanwhile, as a result of the victory of the Republican Left of Catalonia, Francesc Maci\xc3\xa0 proclaimed in Barcelona the Catalan Republic.

  • April 15 \xe2\x80\x93 The Castellammarese War ends with the assassination of Joe "The Boss" Masseria, briefly leaving Salvatore Maranzano as capo di tutti i capi ("boss of all bosses") and undisputed ruler of the American Mafia. Maranzano is himself assassinated less than 6 months later, leading to the establishment of the Five Families.
    • \n
  • April 17 \xe2\x80\x93 After the negotiations between the republican ministers of Spain and Catalonia, the Catalan Republic transforms itself into the Generalitat de Catalunya, an autonomous government inside the Spanish Republic.
    • \n
  • April 22 \xe2\x80\x93 Austria, Britain, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Sweden and the USA recognize the Spanish Republic.

  • April 25 \xe2\x80\x93 The automobile manufacturer Porsche is founded by Ferdinand Porsche in Stuttgart.
