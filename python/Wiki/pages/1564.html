Year 1564 (MDLXIV) was a leap year starting on Saturday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 26 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Ula (Livonian War): A Lithuanian surprise attack results in a decisive defeat of the numerically superior Russian forces.
    • \n
  • March 25 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Angol in Chile: Spanish Conquistador Lorenzo Bernal del Mercado defeats and kills the toqui Illanguli\xc3\xa9n.
    • \n
  • June 22 \xe2\x80\x93 French settlers abandon Charlesfort, the first French attempt at colonizing what is now the United States, and establish Fort Caroline in Florida.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July \xe2\x80\x93 English merchant Anthony Jenkinson returns to London from his second expedition to the Grand Duchy of Moscow, having gained a considerable extension of trading rights for the English Muscovy Company.
    • \n
  • September 10 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Kawanakajima in Japan: Takeda Shingen fights the forces of Uesugi Kenshin for the final time.
    • \n
  • November 21 \xe2\x80\x93 Spanish Conquistador Miguel L\xc3\xb3pez de Legazpi sails from Mexico. Later, he will conquer the Philippine Islands, founding Manila.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • First recorded report of a \'rat king\'.[1]
    • \n
  • approx. date \xe2\x80\x93 Idris Alooma starts to rule the Kanem-Bornu Empire.
    • \n
