1993 (MCMXCIII) was a common year starting on Friday (dominical letter C) of the Gregorian calendar, the 1993rd year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 993rd year of the 2nd millennium, the 93rd year of the 20th century, and the 4th year of the 1990s decade.

January[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 1\n

    \n
    • \n
  • January 5\n

    • The state of Washington executes Westley Allan Dodd by hanging (the first judicial hanging in America since 1965).

Four men, Samuel Millar, Father Patrick Moloney, former Rochester Police officer Thomas O\'Connor, and Charles McCormick, all of whom have ties to the Provisional Irish Republican Army, are accused.

    • \n

March[edit]

\n
    \n
  • March 4 \xe2\x80\x93 Authorities announce the capture of suspected World Trade Center bombing conspirator Mohammad Salameh.

      \n
    • Jiang Zemin becomes President of the People\'s Republic of China.

      • \n
    • Mahamane Ousmane is elected president of Niger.
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • March 28 \xe2\x80\x93 French legislative election, 1993: Rally for the Republic (Gaullist party) wins a majority and \xc3\x89douard Balladur becomes Prime Minister.
