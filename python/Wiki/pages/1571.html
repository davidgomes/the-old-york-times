Year 1571 (MDLXXI) was a common year starting on Monday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 11 \xe2\x80\x93 The Austrian nobility are granted freedom of religion.
    • \n
  • January 23 \xe2\x80\x93 The Royal Exchange opens in London, England.
    • \n
  • March 18 \xe2\x80\x93 The Order of the Knights of Saint John transfer the capital of Malta from Birgu to Valletta.
    • \n
  • May 24 \xe2\x80\x93 Fire of Moscow: Moscow is burnt by the Crimean army under Devlet I Giray.
    • \n
  • June 3 \xe2\x80\x93 Following conquest of the Kingdom of Maynila, Spanish Conquistador Miguel L\xc3\xb3pez de Legazpi makes Manila a city and the capital of the Philippines.
    • \n
  • June 25 \xe2\x80\x93 Queen Elizabeth\'s Grammar School, Horncastle, is founded in Lincolnshire, England.
    • \n
  • June 27 \xe2\x80\x93 Establishment of Jesus College "within the City and University of Oxford of Queen Elizabeth\'s foundation" in England by Welsh cleric and lawyer Hugh Price.[1]
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 25 \xe2\x80\x93 St Olave\'s Grammar School is founded in Tooley Street, London.
    • \n
  • August 1 \xe2\x80\x93 Ottoman conquest of Cyprus is concluded by the surrender of Famagusta. Cyprus is established as an eyalet of the Ottoman Empire and the first Turkish colony moves into the island.
    • \n
  • August 29 \xe2\x80\x93 Foundation of Liliw, Laguna, Philippines, by Gat Tayaw, followers and residents as a municipality of Laguna.
    • \n
  • September 28 \xe2\x80\x93 The House of Commons of England introduces the first pro forma bill, symbolizing its authority over its own affairs.[2]
    • \n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\nBattle of Lepanto, 1571
\n
\n
\n
    \n
  • October 7 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Lepanto: Spanish, Venetian, and Papal naval forces under Don John of Austria defeat the Turkish fleet of Ali Monizindade Pasha.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • Using mercury in the silver extraction process dramatically increases the output of the Potos\xc3\xad mine; thus begins the great silver flow that links the New and Old Worlds.[3]
    • \n
