Year 1574 (MDLXXIV) was a common year starting on Friday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • February 23 \xe2\x80\x93 The fifth War of Religion against the Huguenots begins in France.
    • \n
  • April 14 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Mookerheyde: Spanish forces under Sancho de Avila defeat the rebel forces of Louis of Nassau, who is killed.
    • \n
  • May 30 \xe2\x80\x93 On the death of King Charles IX of France of France, he is succeeded by his brother King Henry of Poland, who becomes King Henry III of France. His mother, Catherine de\' Medici, acts as Regent until Henry arrives from Poland.
    • \n
  • June 10 \xe2\x80\x93 Manila gains cityhood.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • August 30 \xe2\x80\x93 Guru Ram Das becomes the fourth of the Sikh gurus.
    • \n
  • October 3 \xe2\x80\x93 The city of Leiden, besieged by the Spanish, is relieved by a Sea Beggars fleet under Louis Boisot.
    • \n
  • November 22 \xe2\x80\x93 The Juan Fern\xc3\xa1ndez Islands in the South Pacific Ocean are discovered by Spanish sailor Juan Fern\xc3\xa1ndez.
    • \n
  • November 29 \xe2\x80\x93 Limahong and Juan de Salcedo quarrel during the Battle of Manila.
    • \n
  • December \xe2\x80\x93 Murad III succeeds Selim II as Ottoman Emperor.
    • \n
\n

Undated[edit]

\n
    \n
  • Prince El-Mirza of Kakheti is defeated in his bid for the throne by his half-brother, Alexander II.
    • \n
  • La Alameda, Seville, is laid out in Spain as Europe\'s first public garden.[1]
    • \n
\n
