Year 1554 (MDLIV) was a common year starting on Monday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 5 \xe2\x80\x93 A great fire breaks out in Eindhoven, Netherlands.
    • \n
  • January 12 \xe2\x80\x93 Bayinnaung is crowned king of the Burmese Taungoo dynasty.
    • \n
  • January 25 \xe2\x80\x93 S\xc3\xa3o Paulo, Brazil, is founded.
    • \n
  • February 9 \xe2\x80\x93 Thomas Wyatt surrenders to government forces in London.[1]
    • \n
  • February 12 \xe2\x80\x93 After claiming the throne of England the previous year, Lady Jane Grey is beheaded for treason.
    • \n
  • March 18 \xe2\x80\x93 Princess Elizabeth is imprisoned in the Tower of London.
    • \n
  • April 12 \xe2\x80\x93 Mary of Guise becomes Regent of Scotland.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 23\xe2\x80\x93July 25 \xe2\x80\x93 Queen Mary I of England marries King Philip of Naples, the only son of Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor, in Winchester, England.
    • \n
  • August 2 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Marciano: Senese\xe2\x80\x93French forces are defeated by the Florentine\xe2\x80\x93Imperial army.
    • \n
  • August 12 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Renty: French forces led by Francis, Duke of Guise turn back an invasion of Picardy by Charles V.
    • \n
  • November \xe2\x80\x93 English captain John Lok voyages to Guinea.[2][3]
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • Mikael Agricola becomes the bishop of Turku.
    • \n
  • Saadi conquer the Kingdom of Fez.
    • \n
  • Exact center year of Counter Reformation.
    • \n
  • Name of beer brewed by New Belgium Brewing Company based on a recipe from this date called "1554."
