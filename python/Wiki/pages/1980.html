1980 (MCMLXXX) was a leap year starting on Tuesday (dominical letter FE) of the Gregorian calendar, the 1980th year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 980th year of the 2nd millennium, the 80th year of the 20th century, and the 1st year of the 1980s decade.

January[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 1 \xe2\x80\x93 Changes to the Swedish Act of Succession make Princess Victoria of Sweden first in line to the throne ("heir apparent") and therefore Crown Princess, ahead of her younger brother.

    • \n
  • January 20 \xe2\x80\x93 Cuba recognizes the Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic (SADR).
    • \n
  • January 21\n
      \n

      • \n

  • January 26 \xe2\x80\x93 Israel and Egypt establish diplomatic relations.[1]
    • \n
  • January 27 \xe2\x80\x93 Canadian Caper: Six United States diplomats, posing as Canadians, manage to escape from Tehran, Iran as they board a flight to Z\xc3\xbcrich, Switzerland on Swissair.
