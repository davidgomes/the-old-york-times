Year 1533 (MDXXXIII) was a common year starting on Wednesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 25 \xe2\x80\x93 King Henry VIII of England marries Anne Boleyn, who becomes his second queen consort.
    • \n
  • January 26 \xe2\x80\x93 Thomas Audley, 1st Baron Audley of Walden, is appointed Lord Chancellor of England
    • \n
  • March 30 \xe2\x80\x93 Thomas Cranmer becomes Archbishop of Canterbury.
    • \n
  • May 23 \xe2\x80\x93 King Henry VIII of England\'s marriage with Catherine of Aragon is declared annulled by Archbishop Cranmer. Since Pope Clement VII had rejected Henry\'s petition for annulment in 1530, Catherine continues to believe herself Henry\'s wife until her death.
    • \n
  • June 1 \xe2\x80\x93 Cranmer crowns Anne Boleyn as queen consort of England in Westminster Abbey.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 11 \xe2\x80\x93 Henry VIII is excommunicated by Pope Clement VII, as is Archbishop Cranmer.
    • \n
  • July 22 \xe2\x80\x93 Treaty of Constantinople between the Ottoman Empire and the Archduchy of Austria: Ferdinand I, King of the Romans, withdraws his claims to most of Hungary and J\xc3\xa1nos Szapolyai, voivode of Transylvania, becomes King of Hungary under the suzerainty of Suleiman the Magnificent, Sultan of the Ottoman Empire.
    • \n
  • July 26 \xe2\x80\x93 Sapa Inca Atahualpa is executed by garotte at the orders of Francisco Pizarro in Cajamarca. The Spanish arrange for his younger brother T\xc3\xbapac Huallpa to be crowned as a successor, but he dies of smallpox soon afterwards
    • \n
  • September 7 \xe2\x80\x93 Anne Boleyn gives birth to Princess Elizabeth, the future Elizabeth I of England.
    • \n
  • November 15 \xe2\x80\x93 Francisco Pizarro arrives in Cusco, Peru.
    • \n
  • December 3 \xe2\x80\x93 Ivan IV succeeds his father Vasili III as Grand Prince of Muscovy at the age of three years.
    • \n
  • December \xe2\x80\x93 Hernando de Grijalva and his crew discover the uninhabited Revillagigedo Islands off the Pacific coast of Mexico\n
      \n
    • December 21 (Feast of St Thomas the Apostle) \xe2\x80\x93 They discover Isla Santo Tom\xc3\xa9, probably Socorro Island.
      • \n
    • December 28 \xe2\x80\x93 They discover Isla de los Inocentes, probably San Benedicto Island.[1]
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • The Statute in Restraint of Appeals declares the king to be the supreme sovereign in England and forbids judicial appeals to the papacy.
    • \n
  • Paracelsus interprets the Bible in Appenzell.
    • \n
  • Pechenga Monastery is founded in the far north of Russia.
    • \n
  • 1533\xe2\x80\x931534 \xe2\x80\x93 Sultan Suleiman the Magnificent makes the Ruthenian harem girl Roxelana his legal wife.
    • \n
\n
