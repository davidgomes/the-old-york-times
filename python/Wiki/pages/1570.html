Year 1570 (MDLXX) was a common year starting on Sunday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 9 \xe2\x80\x93 Ivan the Terrible starts the Massacre of Novgorod.
    • \n
  • January 23 \xe2\x80\x93 The assassination of Scottish regent James Stewart, 1st Earl of Moray, by James Hamilton (the first recorded assassination using a firearm) throws Scotland into civil war.
    • \n
  • February 8 \xe2\x80\x93 A magnitude 8.3 earthquake occurs in Concepci\xc3\xb3n, Chile.
    • \n
  • February 15 \xe2\x80\x93 Venus occults Jupiter; this will next happen in 1818.
    • \n
  • February 25 \xe2\x80\x93 Pope Pius V excommunicates Queen Elizabeth I of England with the bull Regnans in Excelsis.
    • \n
  • May 20 \xe2\x80\x93 Abraham Ortelius publishes the first modern atlas, Theatrum Orbis Terrarum, in Antwerp.
    • \n
  • June 10 \xe2\x80\x93 The Kingdom of Livonia is established.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 3 \xe2\x80\x93 Ottoman conquest of Cyprus begins.
    • \n
  • July 14 \xe2\x80\x93 Pope Pius V issues Quo primum, promulgating the 1570 edition of the Roman Missal.
    • \n
  • July 30 \xe2\x80\x93 The Sengoku period nihongo Battle of Anegawa \xe5\xa7\x89\xe5\xb7\x9d\xe3\x81\xae\xe6\x88\xa6\xe3\x81\x84 Ane-gawa no Tatakai occurred near Lake Biwa in \xc5\x8cmi Province, Japan, between the allied forces of Oda Nobunaga and Tokugawa Ieyasu, against the combined forces of the Azai and Asakura clans.
    • \n
  • August 8 \xe2\x80\x93 The Peace of Saint-Germain ends the Third War of Religion in France. Again, the Huguenots are promised religious freedom and political autonomy.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • Spanish conquistador Juan de Salcedo in the service of Miguel L\xc3\xb3pez de Legazpi begins conquest of the Kingdom of Maynila.
    • \n
  • Construction of the original Catedral Nuestra Se\xc3\xb1ora de La Asunci\xc3\xb3n, the oldest church in Venezuela, begins.
    • \n
  • Whitechapel Bell Foundry known to be in existence in London. By the early 21st century it will be the oldest manufacturing company in Great Britain.
    • \n
  • Andrea Palladio publishes I quattro libri dell\'architettura in Venice.
    • \n
  • The Andean population of Peru reaches 1.3 million.
    • \n
\n
