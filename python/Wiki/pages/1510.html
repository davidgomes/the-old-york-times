Year 1510 (MDX) was a common year starting on Tuesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January \xe2\x80\x93 Catherine of Aragon gives birth to her first child, a stillborn daughter.
    • \n
  • January 23 \xe2\x80\x93 An 18-year-old Henry VIII of England jousts anonymously at Richmond, Surrey and draws applause before revealing himself.
    • \n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\nPortuguese Armada retreat after monsoon conditions and a counterattack by the forces of Yusuf Adil Shah, ruler of the Bijapur Sultanate.
\n
\n
\n
    \n
  • February 27 \xe2\x80\x93 Afonso de Albuquerque of Portugal conquers Goa.
    • \n
  • May 12 \xe2\x80\x93 The Prince of Anhua rebellion begins when Zhu Zhifan, Prince of Anhua, kills all the officials invited to a banquet and declares his intent on ousting the powerful Ming dynasty eunuch Liu Jin during the reign of the Zhengde Emperor in China.
    • \n
  • May 30 \xe2\x80\x93 Rebel leader Zhu Zhifan is defeated and captured by commander Qiu Yue, ending the Prince of Anhua rebellion.
    • \n
  • July \xe2\x80\x93 The Holy League, formed to defend the Italian States, attacks French-occupied Genoa.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • The Grand Prince of Moscow Vasili III conquers Pskov
    • \n
  • Peter Henlein builds the first pocket watch.
    • \n
  • Sir Thomas More becomes undersheriff of the City of London.
    • \n
  • Paolo Cortese publishes De Cardinalatu, a manual for cardinals, including advice on palatial architecture \xe2\x80\x93 which inspires Thomas Wolsey in his construction work at Hampton Court Palace.
    • \n
  • Heinrich Cornelius Agrippa moves to Italy.
    • \n
  • Sunflowers are brought to Europe.
    • \n
