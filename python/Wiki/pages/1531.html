Year 1531 (MDXXXI) was a common year starting on Sunday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 26 \xe2\x80\x93 Lisbon, Portugal is hit by an earthquake in which thousands die.
    • \n
  • February 27 \xe2\x80\x93 Lutheran princes in the Holy Roman Empire form an alliance known as the Schmalkaldic League.
    • \n
  • February or March \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Antukyah: Ahmad ibn Ibrahim al-Ghazi of the Adal Sultanate defeats the Ethiopian army.
    • \n
  • April \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Pun\xc3\xa1: Francisco Pizarro defeats the island\'s native inhabitants.
    • \n
  • April 16 \xe2\x80\x93 The city of Puebla, Mexico, is founded.
    • \n
  • June 24 \xe2\x80\x93 The city of San Juan del R\xc3\xado, Mexico, is founded.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 25 \xe2\x80\x93 The city of Santiago de Quer\xc3\xa9taro, Mexico is founded.
    • \n
  • September 22 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Obertyn: The Moldavians are defeated by Polish forces under Jan Tarnowski, allowing the Poles to recapture Pokucie.
    • \n
  • October 11 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Kappel: The forces of Z\xc3\xbcrich are defeated by the Catholic cantons. Huldrych Zwingli, the Swiss religious reformer, is killed.
    • \n
  • October 28 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Amba Sel: Imam Ahmad ibn Ibrahim al-Ghazi again defeats the army of Lebna Dengel, Emperor of Ethiopia. The southern part of Ethiopia thus falls under Imam Ahmad\'s control.
    • \n
  • December 12 \xe2\x80\x93 The Blessed Virgin Mary, in the guise of Our Lady of Guadalupe, appears imprinted on the tilm\xc3\xa0tli of Juan Diego Cuauhtlatoatzin, an Aztec convert to Catholicism, in Tepeyacac near Mexico City.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • Andrea Alciato publishes the first part of his Emblemata.
    • \n
  • Comet Halley appears (perihelion: August 26).
    • \n
  • Conquistador Francisco de Montejo claims Chichen Itza as capital of Spanish-ruled Yucat\xc3\xa1n.
    • \n
  • The University of Sarajevo is founded by Gazi Husrev-beg.
    • \n
  • K\xc3\xb5pu Lighthouse is completed.
    • \n
  • An enormous drought in Henan province, China, coupled with a gigantic swarm of locusts in the summer, forces many in destitute agricultural communities to turn to cannibalism instead of dying by starvation.
    • \n
  • Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor abolishes the worst abuses of the encomienda system by pressure of Bartolom\xc3\xa9 de las Casas.
    • \n
  • Witch-hunt in the town of Schiltach, Germany
    • \n
