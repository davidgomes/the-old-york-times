Year 1515 (MDXV) was a common year starting on Monday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

\n

Events[edit]

\n

January\xe2\x80\x93June[edit]

\n
    \n
  • January 25 \xe2\x80\x93 Coronation of Francis I of France (r. until 1547).
    • \n
  • May 13 \xe2\x80\x93 Mary Tudor, Queen of France and Charles Brandon, 1st Duke of Suffolk are officially married at Greenwich.
    • \n
\n

July\xe2\x80\x93December[edit]

\n
    \n
  • July 2 \xe2\x80\x93 Manchester Grammar School is endowed by Hugh Oldham, the first free grammar school in England.
    • \n
  • July 22 \xe2\x80\x93 At the First Congress of Vienna, a double wedding takes place to cement agreements. Louis, only son of King Vladislaus II of Hungary, marries Mary of Austria, granddaughter of Maximilian I, Holy Roman Emperor; and Mary\'s brother, Archduke Ferdinand, marries Vladislaus\' daughter, Anna.
    • \n
  • August 25 \xe2\x80\x93 Conquistador Diego Vel\xc3\xa1zquez de Cu\xc3\xa9llar founds Havana, Cuba.
    • \n
  • September 13\xe2\x80\x9314 \xe2\x80\x93 Battle of Marignano: The army of Francis I of France defeats the Swiss, thanks to the timely arrival of a Venetian army. Francis restores French control of Milan.
    • \n
  • November 15 \xe2\x80\x93 Thomas Wolsey is invested as a Cardinal.
    • \n
  • December 24 \xe2\x80\x93 Thomas Wolsey is named Lord Chancellor of England.
    • \n
\n

Date unknown[edit]

\n
    \n
  • Ottoman sultan Selim I kills Dulkadirid ruler Ala\xc3\xbcddevle, challenging the Mameluk sultan of Egypt al-Ghawri.
    • \n
  • The Yadigarid Uzbeks found the Khanate of Khiva.
    • \n
  • Bartolom\xc3\xa9 de las Casas urges Ferdinand II of Aragon to end Amerindian slavery and recommends experimental free towns.[1][2]
    • \n
  • D\xc3\xbcrer\'s Rhinoceros cut.
    • \n
  • Henry Cornelius Agrippa returns to Northern Italy.
    • \n
\n
